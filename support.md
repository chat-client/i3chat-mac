## English

Thank you for purchasing i3Chat. If you have any questions or need assistance, please refer to the information below.

### Purchase & Download

- i3Chat is available exclusively on the **Mac App Store**. After purchase, you can re-download it at any time from the Purchased section of the App Store at no additional cost.
- Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID. To use the app on multiple Macs, simply sign in with the same Apple ID.

### Refund Policy

All purchases are subject to **Apple's standard refund policy**. To request a refund, please visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).

### Getting Started

1. Launch i3Chat after installation.
2. Click **Connect** in the login window.
3. Enter your IRC server address, port, nickname, and optionally a channel (e.g., `irc.rizon.net`, port `6667`, channel `#test`).
4. Click **Connect** to join.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use i3Chat with any IRC server?**  
A: Yes. i3Chat supports standard IRC servers. Enter the server hostname and port in the connection window.

**Q: Does i3Chat support SSL/TLS connections?**  
A: Yes. Enable the SSL option in the connection window and use the appropriate SSL port (usually `6697`).

**Q: I closed the main window. How do I get it back?**  
A: Use the **Window → Show Main Window** menu item, or press **?0**.

**Q: How do I view chat history?**  
A: Open **History** from the menu to browse past messages stored locally.

### 快速上手

1. 安装完成后启动 i3Chat。
2. 在登录窗口点击**连接**。
3. 填写 IRC 服务器地址、端口、昵称，以及可选频道（例如：`irc.rizon.net`，端口 `6667`，频道 `#test`）。
4. 点击**连接**即可加入聊天。

### 常见问题

**Q：i3Chat 支持哪些 IRC 服务器？**  
A：支持所有标准 IRC 服务器，在连接窗口填写服务器地址和端口即可。

**Q：支持 SSL/TLS 加密连接吗？**  
A：支持。在连接窗口勾选 SSL 选项，并使用对应端口（通常为 `6697`）。

**Q：主窗口关掉了怎么找回？**  
A：使用菜单栏 **窗口 → 显示主窗口**，或按快捷键 **?0**。

**Q：如何查看历史记录？**  
A：在菜单中打开**历史**窗口，可浏览本地保存的聊天记录。
